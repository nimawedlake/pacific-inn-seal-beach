Welcome

Orange County's
Best-Kept Secret

Nestled in the heart of charming Seal Beach, The Pacific Inn delivers the warmth and attentive service of a boutique hotel alongside the amenities of a full-service resort. Just blocks from the sand, the 1,865-foot pier, and the vibrant shops and cafes of Main Street.

Since 1986, we've welcomed guests to experience the unhurried beauty of the Southern California coast. Every one of our 71 freshly renovated rooms features a private balcony, so you can soak in the mild ocean air any time of day.
